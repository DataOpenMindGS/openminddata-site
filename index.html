WHAT WE DO
Alternative data. Institutional quality.

Founded by veterans of financial data, systematic investing, and business development, Open Mind Data is built on one core belief: the most valuable signals are the ones the market hasn't fully priced in yet. We find those signals, structure them, and deliver them in formats that serious investors can actually use.

Our work spans multiple asset classes and data categories — from corporate capital allocation behavior to emerging market intelligence from prediction markets.

With backgrounds spanning Bridgewater Associates, Reuters, S&P Capital IQ, Edgar Online, and Value Line, our team brings decades of experience in financial data discovery, productization, and institutional distribution.

OUR APPROACH
Why alternative data matters now

Traditional financial data — earnings, fundamentals, analyst estimates — is widely available, well-priced, and fully arbitraged. The edge has shifted to datasets that are harder to find, harder to structure, and harder to interpret.

That is precisely where Open Mind Data operates. We bring the discipline of institutional data management to emerging and underutilized data categories, turning raw signals into clean, queryable intelligence that fits into existing investment workflows.
